Vaathi

Vaathi (titled Sir in Telugu) is a 2023 Indian period action drama film in which actor Dunush plays the titulat role of an assistant school teacher. The story,set in the 1990s,revolves around his chatacter's fight against the privatization and commercialization of the education system. Thani Oruvan

Thani Oruvan is a critically acclaimed 2015 Tamil action thriller film that follows an upright IPS officer named Mithran (Jayam Ravi) as he tries to expose the corrupt and influential scientist Siddharth Abhimanyu (Arvind Swamy). The movie is known for its intelligent screenplay and a compelling cat-and-mouse chase between the protagonist and the stylish, charismatic antagonist. It was a major commercial success and received praise for the performances, direction, and unique approach to the typical good vs. evil theme. Jailer

Jailer is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action comedy film about a retired jailer named Muthuvel Pandian who is forced to revisit his violent past to protect his family when his police officer son goes missing while investigating an idol smuggling mafia. Style Known for its racy screenplay, high-octane action sequences, and Anirudh Ravichander's exhilarating background music, the movie was praised for presenting Rajinikanth in a stylish and charismatic new light. Vikram

Vikram is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and part of the Lokesh Cinematic Universe (LCU). The story follows a former black-ops commander, played by Kamal Haasan, who goes undercover to dismantle a massive drug syndicate after his son is murdered.

The film also stars Fahadh Faasil as a special investigator and Vijay Sethupathi as a ruthless drug lord, with a notable cameo by Suriya as the syndicate's elusive kingpin, Rolex. Praised for its intense action sequences, gripping narrative, and high-octane background score by Anirudh Ravichander, Vikram became one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of all time and set the stage for future installments in the shared universe.
